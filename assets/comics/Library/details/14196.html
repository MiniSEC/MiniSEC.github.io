52 - Thief / The History Of The Dcu Part 7
6/28/2006  First Printing
Comic  Modern Age $2.50
Read It: Yes 32 Pages

John Henry Irons confirms that he's been exposed to Luthor's metagene therapy against his will. Before he can explain this to Natasha, she hears the test results, and reacts with a combination of fatigue, frustration, and misunderstanding. Meanwhile, the Space Heroes learn what's been waiting for them, and Booster Gold's life gets a little bit worse.
