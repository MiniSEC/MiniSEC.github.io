52 - Haystack / The Origin Of Elongated Man
8/2/2006
Comic  Modern Age $2.50
Read It: Yes 32 Pages

The Cult of Connor is ready to attempt their resurrection of Susan Dibny as a trial-run for their plan to resurrect Superboy. Ralph Dibny has called in a few friends who have some experience dealing with the afterlife to help him determine whether or not this ceremony might actually succeed, or if this is some kind of bizarre con. Meanwhile, Isis continues to have a profound affect on Black Adam, as he busts up a child-slavery ring while looking for her young brother.
