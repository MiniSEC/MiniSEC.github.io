52 - Uhebbuki / The Origin Of Black Adam
October 2006
Comic  Modern Age $2.50
Read It: Yes 32 Pages

Renee and Vic have spent the past week living out of a cargo container and trying to dodge the Khandaq authorities while also trying to figure out what Intergang is planning. But as the week opens, dodging the authorities is much easier than usual. Black Adam has asked Isis for her hand in marriage, and the entire nation is swept up in the celebration as she has accepted. The entire Marvel family is in Khandaq for the event, as Captain Marvel himself will perform the Wedding. Montoya deduces that Intergang is planning to use a suicide bomber in the crowd to disrupt the ceremony. Can she and Vic find the bomber in time to prevent a massacre?
