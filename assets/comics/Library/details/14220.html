52 - History Repeats / The Origin Of Animal Man
November 2006
Comic  Modern Age $2.50
Read It: Yes 40 Pages

Lobo leads Adam Strange, Starfire and Animal Man to a refuge where they can get out of their ship and stretch their legs near the Vegan Frontier in Sector 3500. Here, he's addressed as Archbishop Lobo of the First Celestial Church of the Triple Fish-God. And though he claims money and thrills are behind him, he's willing to allow them to tag along on his pilgrammage to the Three Golden Planets, if they have the stomach for the trip.Wonder Girl returns to the super-hero scene, where she meets up with Supernova for the first time, and develops her own idea of who is behind the mask.And Skeets recruits Daniel Jon Carter, Booster Gold's ancestor, to make another trip into Rip Hunter's lab. Daniel's DNA is a close enough match that Booster's gear will work for him, and Skeets wants to use it to remotely see what was down in that lab. Apparently Booster said there was 'nothing' there.
