52 - Teambuilding Exercises
November 2006
Comic  Modern Age $2.50
Read It: Yes 36 Pages

Lex Luthor's new super-team is ready for their big debut. They've been assigned codenames, and the stage has been set. But before their first public confrontation, Natasha goes to Lex to ask him if he'll allow Eliza Harmon to re-join the team. She's cleaned up her act, and dealt with her drug habit. Lex allows it, and the new Infinity Inc makes a big splash battling the new Blockbuster in Las Vegas. After they've apprehended him, the Teen Titans arrive on the scene. But without Wonder Girl, Robin, Kid Flash, Superboy or Cyborg, can they really call themselves the Teen Titans? The two groups bristle a little at each other, as the Titans resent Luthor's team using the name Infinity Inc, as well as some of the associated code-names.But Lex is a master manipulator, and he has an idea to turn this meeting into an event to launch his team into the big time.
