52 - Liminal Times / The Origin Of Nightwing
December 2006
Comic  Modern Age $2.50
Read It: Yes 40 Pages

Things are afoot in multiple avenues. Checkmate is back in action under U.N. jurisdiction, and they've selected their new White King. Intergang is making a strong play for Gotham. The new Black Marvel family is out in the public eye. Ralph Dibney is still searching for a way to bring Sue back. Infinity Inc. has a new active member. Dr. Magnus has learned where all of the missing scientists have gone, and he's not entirely thrilled with their offer.
