Countdown - Family Feud / The Origin Of General Zod
10/3/2007
Comic  Modern Age $2.99
Read It: Yes 32 Pages

Meeting Brother Eye, Karate Kid is informed that he is host to a viral infection.At Green Arrow and Black Canary's wedding, Pied Piper and Trcikster watch from the sidelines as the gate-crashing villains are dealt with before they make their escape.In Cadmus, the experiments to find out what is happening to Jimmy Olsen goes wrong, turning Olsen into two separate forms - an elastic, shapeshifter and a spirit form; realising he needs to leave before he accidentally kills someone, Olsen manages to squeeze through a drain cover into the sewers.On Earth 15, the Challengers From Beyond, along with the Jokester, meet their counterparts and are told that Ray Palmer is not on that Earth.Off the coast of Themyscria, Athena arranges a trial for her would-be Amazons - swim to shore through waters infested with giant sharks.
