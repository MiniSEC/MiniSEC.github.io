Countdown - Girls Gone Wild / The Origin Of Eclipso
9/19/2007
Comic  Modern Age $2.99
Read It: Yes 32 Pages

As Mary Marvel agrees to give Klarion a small piece of her power, he attempts to take more, forcing her to fight him before leaving, following a mysterious voice. Trickster and Pied Piper attempt to escape the room guarded by Zatanna's tame Egyptian mummy with Piper managing to send him to sleep using the tones on a touchpad telephone. Jimmy Olsen is taken to Cadmus where he meets the head of genetics, Serling Roquette. At Black Canary's bachelorette party, Wonder Girl and Supergirl attempt to get hold of some alcohol but are stopped at every turn until the party is accidentally crashed by Piper and Trickster, chased by the now revived mummy. As the assembled heroines attack the mummy, Wonder Girl and Supergirl finally get some drinks only to find they hate the taste of them. As Zatanna makes her mummy vanish, Piper and Trickster escape. The Challengers from Beyond pass through Earth 30 before arriving on Earth 3 where they are confronted by the Crime Society.Mary Marvel arrives in Turkey, following the coive, to find that it belongs to Eclipso.
