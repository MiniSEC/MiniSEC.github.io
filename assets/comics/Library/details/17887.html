The pieces are moving into place for a final confrontation. Hank Pym oversees the creation of new super teams for the 50 State Initiative, the Punisher breaks into the Baxter Building to get the plans to 42, and Sue Storm heads up a recruitment drive looking for support. The unregistered heroes converge on 42, only to be met by Tony's team, who have had a mole on Cap's side for over a week.
