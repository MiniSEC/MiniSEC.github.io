Civil War - Civil War, Part Seven
January 2007
Comic  Modern Age $2.99
Read It: Yes 40 Pages

The final confrontation is at hand. Cap's side has headed into 42 and released all prisoners, greatly boosting their numbers. The fight escapes the prison, and continues into New York, where both sides pull together additional support from other factions. The winner of the conflict isn't clear, but one thing that is known for sure is that it's going to be one heck of a fight.
