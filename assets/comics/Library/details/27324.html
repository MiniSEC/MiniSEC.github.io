The Goldilocks Syndrome (Or: Who's Been Sleeping In My Head?)
March 1983
Comic  Modern Age $0.60
Read It: Yes 32 Pages

The X-Men return home and find the mansion populated with new students. Not recognizing the X-Men, nor realizing that they are here to fight the Brood growing within Charles Xavier, the New Mutants fight the X-Men. They soon understand the true purpose of the mission and team up with their elders, who brought along Starjammer medical technology.
