Following the battle on Muir Island, and in the absence of Charles Xavier, Cyclops has declared the X-Men disbanded. The X-Men served Xavier's dream, and without Xavier, he doesn't know what the purpose of the mutant super-heroes is.But his reverie in the Savage Land with Emma is brought to an early conclusion when Angel reports something strange happening in San Francisco.
