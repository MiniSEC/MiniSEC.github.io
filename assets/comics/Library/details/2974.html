S.H.I.E.L.D. deploys a unit specifically designed to stop the New Warriors. While evading them, the team finally learns Donyell's plan: build a time machine, prevent the Stamford Disaster, and pre-emptively save their dead teammates. They are forced to go through time before the system can be properly tested, though.
