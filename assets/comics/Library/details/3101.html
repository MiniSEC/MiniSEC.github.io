When the Marvel Illuminati decided to shoot the Hulk into space, they were not all gathered together. Professor Charles Xavier was not present for that meeting. And now, the Hulk has returned. Upon receiving the news, the New X-Men want nothing more than to leap into action, either to fight the Hulk or aid in the evacuation of New York City. As the Beast tries to talk them out of it, the shaking of the Earth forces him to realize that discussion is moot. The Hulk has arrived at Xavier's mansion, and he wants to know if Charles would have voted to shoot him into space.
