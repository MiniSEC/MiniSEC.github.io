Resurrection - The X-Men journey to the Savage Land, where they find devastation. They work with the locals to try and restore it, encountering both Terminus and the High Evolutionary along the way.

I Want My X-Men - With Psylocke's eyes no longer broadcasting, Mojo has lost his greatest hit. He soon finds a replacement team to take over the broadcast: the X-Babies.

Demon Night - The High Evolutionary defends his forces, while Bova guides Magda through the birth of some very prominent twins.
