52 - New World Order / The History Of The Dcu Part 2

Power Girl pursues Terra Man into Kahndaq airspace, only to be stopped by Black Adam. The pair exchange words before Power Girl leaves. Black Adam is joined by Terra Man in a press conference that clearly demonstrates his future plans. Meanwhile, Booster Gold looks for a solution for Skeets' malfunctions.
