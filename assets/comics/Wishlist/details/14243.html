52 - Rain Of The Supermen
1/3/2007
Comic  Modern Age $2.50
Read It: Yes 32 Pages

At the touch of a button, Lex Luthor has stripped his Everymen of their powers, with the exceptions of Infinity Inc. He claims it is a matter of genetic issues that hadn't been previously recognized, but Natasha is not ready to believe him, particularly after her uncle's warning. Meanwhile, the heroes in space have come to a disturbing discovery of their own.
