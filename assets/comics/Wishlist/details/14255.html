52 - Trigger Effect / The Origin Of Green Arrow
April 2007
Comic  Modern Age $2.50
Read It: Yes 32 Pages

In Nanda Parbat, Renee Montoya sees a vision of herself as the new Question. Ralph Dibny's story in 52 comes to an end as, in Dr Fate's Tower in Salem, he reveals that he knew all along that Felix Faust was masquerading as the Helmet of Fate. He engineers Neron's appearance and goads him into killing Dibny. The spell of binding that Dibny had cast, however, cannot be removed without his permission, thus trapping both Neron and Faust in the Tower.
