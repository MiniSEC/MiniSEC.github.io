Depressed over the outcome of the Black Marvel's fight with the Suicide Squad, Osiris is convinced that the powers he received from Black Adam are tainted. Desperate to make things right, he travels to the Rock of Eternity to meet with Captain Marvel, hoping that he can take Black Adam's power from him.
