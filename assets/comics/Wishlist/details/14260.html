Having learned from Death, the last of the Apokoliptian Four Horsemen, where he was created and by whom, Black Adam rockets to the Isle of Oolong to mete out his vengeance upon the mad scientists working for Intergang. But this gathering of mad scientists are tired of having been pushed around their entire lives. Combining their efforts, they prepare for the arrival of the Black Marvel.
