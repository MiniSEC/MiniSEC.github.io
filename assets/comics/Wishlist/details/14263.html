With Robin's aid, Bruce has reached Nanda Parbat, to recover from the ritual the Ten-Eyed Surgeons put him through. However, back in Gotham, after several failed attempts to divine Batwoman's name through religious rituals, Whisper A'daire and Bruno Mannheim begin to realize how they can track her down. When Montoya returns to Gotham, Kate is missing... but Nightwing is there to help Renee find and rescue Kate.
