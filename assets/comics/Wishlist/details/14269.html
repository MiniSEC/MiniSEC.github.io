52 - Eve Of Destruction / The Origin Of The Justice Society Of America
June 2007
Comic  Modern Age $2.50
Read It: Yes 32 Pages

As the Justice Society attempts to breach Chang Tzu's defenses, they are confronted with the Great Ten, demanding that Oolong Isle is China's sovreign territory, and declaring that if the Justice Society continues to attempt to breach the shields, that they will instigate a war. Meanwhile, Dr. Magnus springs into action, to neutralize Chang Tzu, and have all of his Science Squad (except T. O. Morrow) taken into custody.
