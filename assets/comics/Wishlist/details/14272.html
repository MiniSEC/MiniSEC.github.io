52 - World War Iii
June 2007
Comic  Modern Age $2.50
Read It: Yes 32 Pages

Black Adam has escaped from Oolong Island, and is venting his fury against the entire world. Desperate to find those truly responsible for the death of his family, he crosses over into China, cutting a massive swath of destruction. The Justice Society, and assembled other heroes stand on the border, told that if they cross over into China they commit an act of war, and that missiles will fly. Can China's Great Ten withstand Adam's fury? Or will China fall just as Bialya did before it?
