All hell is breaking loose. Oa is no longer the centre of the Universe, the Rann-Thanagar war is spreading to other worlds, the O.M.A.C. units are running rampant, the magic users have taken a beating, and the JLA is divided by the murder of Maxwell Lord. There are, however, some at an interdimensional crossroads who are not happy with what they can see, and who feel they may be able to fix it all.
