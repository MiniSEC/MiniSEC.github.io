Countdown - Darkseid Equals Death / The Origin Of Darkseid
June 2008
Comic  Modern Age $2.99
Read It: Yes 32 Pages

The Justice League help rescue the civilians of Metropolis as Darkseid and Jimmy Olsen battle, both grown to enormous size, amongst its buildings. Hidden in Jimmy's brain, the Atom discovers the repository of New Gods' souls and, as Darkseid is about to kill Jimmy, takes it out of his head and crushes it, releasing a huge plume of energy. As Darkseid threatens to kill the Atom, Orion appears having obviously survived his apparent death in The Death Of The New Gods #5. The heroes watch as the father and son battle each other to a stalemate until Orion rips out Darkseid's heart from his chest, killing him.
