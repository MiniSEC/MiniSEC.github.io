Countdown - Girls Just Wanna Have Fun / The Origin Of Parallax
8/29/2007
Comic  Modern Age $2.99
Read It: Yes 32 Pages

Donna Troy and her friends have been captured by Queen Belthera of the nanoverse who turns the Atom into an insect.Mary Marvel fights with Zatanna - unknowingly goaded by Eclipso - until Zatanna throws her out of her home.Jimmy Olsen recovers from his tryout with the Justice League and realises that his superpowers are beyond his control.At the Athenian shelter, Holly Robinson is forced to take part in a purification rite that is little more than a gladiatorial competition between her and her fellow homeless women. She manages to defeat all of them until she faces off against a masked opponent.Karate Kid and Una defeat Equus, prompting the arrival of Mr Orr who claims to have the answers Karate Kid needs.Donna Troy manages to escape Belthera's bonds but is then struck down by Bob the Monitor who has fallen under the Queen's spell.
