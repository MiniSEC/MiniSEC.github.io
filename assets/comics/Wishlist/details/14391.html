Karate Kid and Una, refugees from the 31st century seek an audience with Oracle at the suggestion of Starman, but Barbara Gordon's time is a precious commodity. Especially when she's attempting to prevent someone from stealing the secret identities of every superhero on Earth. The Penguin's reception of the Trickster and Pied Piper was initially cold... but it didn't take the old bird long to realize that there was money to be made in those two. Once more on the run from the Suicide Squad, they seek a new place to go to ground.
