As the Trickster and Pied Piper plummet to earth, having leapt out of a plane where they were in custody of a group of villains with a mysterious agenda, the recent violence against the United States prompts people to rise up, protesting outside of the Athenian Womens Shelters around the country. Jason Todd, Donna Troy and Bob the Monitor reach the sub-atomic universe discovered by Ray Palmer, with the aid of the new Atom. But will Ryan Choi be able to help them deal with the locals?
