Final Crisis - Let There Be Lightning
June 2008
Comic  Modern Age $0.50
Read It: Yes 32 Pages

A narrator with a certain level of omniscience is detailing his view of the DC Universe and multiverse. This panoramic snapshot introduces the major players in the world, on both sides of the law, and gives hints towards the narrator's identity and the upcoming Final Crisis.
