The remaining free members of humanity are scattered and beaten. Heroes and villains are joining forces to keep the world alive. Mr. Terrific is coordinating the bulk of the superpowered resistance through multiple Watchtowers, but they're fighting a losing battle. The only person who seems able to combat the effects of the anti-life equation is Barry Allen, but even he may not be fast enough to save everyone.
